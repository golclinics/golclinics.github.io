Data Structures and Algorithms
— from zero to hero.

A Tech Interview Prep program to help students from all walks of life to level up on
data structures, algorithms, system design and other Computer Science Fundamentals.

The training will be a 3 month program; however, there will be an open community that will be running meetups, chat forums, mock-interviews, break-out sessions and many more — indefinitely

Core Topics

I: Foundational Data Structures and Concepts: Arrays and Strings, Stacks and Queues, Linked Lists, Dictionaries and Hash-maps, Big-O Analysis.

II: Foundational Algorithms: Search, Sorting.

III: Advanced Data Structures: Graphs, Trees.

IV. Advanced Algorithms: Greedy Algorithms, Recursion and Backtracking, Dynamic Programming.

01.Entry Challenge

To enter the program, complete the entry challenge by following the instructions here.

02. Challenge Review

Your challenge will be reviewed by our team, and those that meet the selection will be invited to our pilot program. Then the fun begins.

03. Welcome to the Community

Once you accept the invite, you will be added on our various platforms.
