Do you accept the challenge?

This is an open coding challenge for developers of all levels. All you need is just to say yes :)

Prize money, schwag and interview opportunities

Top participants will win prize money, bragging rights, schwag from Developer Circles / Kazi+, and most importantly interview opportunities for internship with top companies in Silicon Valley (for students).

01. Sign Up on Kattis

Create your account on Open Kattis. Make sure to go to settings and set up a proper username and update your country.

02. Sign up for the Challenge

Fill this form here to sign up for the challenge - bit.ly/KChallenge2018. This will help us keep track of your progress

03. Start Coding Away

Kattis has a number of challenges, each with different levels of difficulties. For this challenge, the order at which you do the challenges does not matter, go ahead and code away!

Basic Rules

We don't want to make this complicated, we have very basic rules:

  • The challenge begins May 1 and runs until July 1, 2018
  • Besides having at least 300 points on Kattis, the winners must have solved:
    • at least 5 problems worth at least 3 points
    • at least 3 problems worth at least 4 points, and
    • at least 1 problem worth at least 5 points
  • We will select winners based on the change in scores (and rank) (delta) between the competition's time period. For instance, if person A's score moved from 1 to 300, and person B's score moved from 200 to 400, we will pick person A for prizes.
  • Interview opportunities will be subject to a number of things, and there will be other extra pre-qualifications. But will still go to top competitors in terms of ranking.
  • The aim of this competition is to encourage competitive programming in Kenya; which to a good extent improved someone's software development chops. So take it easy, everyone is a winner as long as you are in it.
  • There will be special prizes for top ladies.
