Data Structures and Algorithmns
— from zero to hero.

A Tech Interview Prep program to help students from all walks of life to level up on
data structures, algorithmns, system design and other Computer Science Fundamentals.

The training will be a 3 month program; however, there will be an open community that will be running meetups, chat forums, mock-interviews, break-out sessions and many more — indefinitely

Core Topics

I: Foundational Data Structures and Concepts: Arrays and Strings, Stacks and Queues, Linked Lists, Dictionaries and Hash-maps, Big-O Analysis.

II: Foundational Algorithms: Search, Sorting.

III: Advanced Data Structures: Graphs, Trees.

IV. Advanced Algorithmns: Greedy Algorithms, Recursion and Backtracking, Dynamic Programming.
